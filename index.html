Fruit Bowl

This fruit bowl or platter is constructed of purely hardwood and glue. The piece measures 22" in diameter and took over 50 hours to complete. The inspiration came from anchient atriums and colluseums.

2018 Summer: Pre-College

During the summer after my junior year of high school I enrolled in the California College of the Arts Industrial Design Pre-College program to diversify my portfolio and make sure that ID was the major I wished to persue.

Cardboard Lounge Chair

This large piece is my take on the renound cardboard chair project, where designers are challenged to create a structural chair from limited matierals. I created a prototype chair and then a full size chair with designed ergonomic contouring and interesting form.

Fractal Lamp

I made this lamp with a laser cutter and some thoughtful CAD work. The final piece was constructed from medium density fiberboard and was decorated with a fractal I coded in grasshopper.
